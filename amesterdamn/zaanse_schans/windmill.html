Paintmill De Kat

windmill
May 18 2:54 PM
Built in 1646, Paintmill De Kat is the last working paint mill in the world, still producing beautiful pigments using age-old methods. As we climbed up the winding stairs, we were greeted with breathtaking panoramic views of the lush countryside and the winding river below. The sunny weather made the views even more precious, illuminating the vibrant greens and blues of the landscape.
